Welcome !

This site is called WanderMust and it caters to serve the wanderlust of travellers interested in seeing the main attraction points of a city. It aims at providing the users with the ‘places to visit’ or the ‘points of interest’ and not just the places like museums and monuments as sometimes even a small coffee shop might be a tourist attraction. So the places shown are ranked by prominence. In addition, the users will also be shown the latest Instagram and Twitter photos for these places.
To get started just enter a city in the search box and happy wandering!
